about us

Founded in 2007, PHAT Returns is now the preferred tax agent for thousands of tax payers.

They recognised a gap in the marketplace for a new, personal and informative approach to income tax returns for all taxpayers, as many accounting practices take an impersonal, one-size-fits-all approach to tax returns. PHAT Return’s efficient approach makes tax returns easier, more enjoyable and outstanding value for money.

Our Tax Specialists have a high level of experience across all industries, including specialised knowledge with the ADF and Travel Agents. This ensures you get the maximum tax return every year.

By utilising all available technology (mobile app, phone, fax, email, SMS and the web) we don’t need to waste your valuable time with a face to face consultation. Value for money is one of the most important factors considered when purchasing a good or service.

The PHAT Returns Tax Tools App is now available for download. The tools include a receipt manager and a logbook for work related kilometres. Just use our unique access code 'PHAT'.

As Registered Tax Agents, you can be assured that our Training and Knowledge of the Australian Taxation System is second to none.
