Frequently Asked Questions

Our tax consultants have a high level of knowledge across many industries so they get you the maximum refund. They also save you time as all bookings are completed over the phone.

After making a booking, all you need to do is fill in the pre booking form at least 24 hours prior to your booking. A PHAT Tax Specialist will then call you at your booking time and complete the tax return.

A booking for one tax return is for 30 minutes.

Our fees are based on what is involved in the process of preparing the tax return for lodgement. A Simple tax return is $99 if you have no deductions. The fees for a Standard tax return usually range between $199 and $299 for each tax return, or $349 to $474 for a tax return with an investment property. Our fee structure is outlined at the start of the booking, and the exact fee will be determined by the Tax Specialist at the completion of the tax return.
