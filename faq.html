Frequently Asked Questions

Our tax consultants have a high level of knowledge across many industries so they get you the maximum refund. They also save you time as all bookings are completed over the phone.

After making an appointment through our booking service, all you need to do is fill in a PHAT form and fax or email it to us with your PAYG summary. A PHAT Consultant will then call you at your booking time and complete the tax return.

A booking for one tax return is for 30 minutes, and one tax return with an investment property is 60 minutes.

Our fees are based on what is involved in the process of preparing the tax return for lodgement. Fees usually range between $169 and $299 for each tax return, or $319 to $474 for a tax return with an investment property. Our fee structure is outlined at the start of the booking, and the exact fee will be determined by the Tax Specialist at the completion of the tax return.
